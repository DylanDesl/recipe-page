Simple Omlette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for ant meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meat.

Preperation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preperation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

    • Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs
